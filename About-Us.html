About Us

Welcome to ResinArtFinland.fi! We are passionate artists and designers dedicated to creating contemporary artworks and interior pieces that seamlessly blend wood, stone, flowers, seashells, and the mesmerizing beauty of the ocean and its waves.

Each of our creations is more than just an art piece—it is a harmonious fusion of nature and modern living, bringing freshness and vitality into your home.

With extensive experience in crafting handmade products upon request, we take pride in delivering unique, high-quality artworks. Our well-established workshop in Vietnam ensures the capability to fulfill orders of all sizes, from small decorative pieces to large-scale interior designs.

At ResinArtFinland.fi, we aspire to bring the wonders of nature into contemporary living spaces, offering artistic creations that evoke inspiration and serenity. We deeply appreciate the support of art lovers in Finland and across Europe, which fuels our passion for continuous innovation.

Join us on this artistic journey and discover the perfect harmony between art and nature with ResinArtFinland.fi.

Dream your artwork bright, we'll bring it to light!
